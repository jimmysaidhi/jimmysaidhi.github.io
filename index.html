Background

Client Name: Buddhist Church of Florin
Business: Church that serves the need of the general public
Problem to be solved: Website was outdated, needed an update in order to make it more modern

Scope

Describe the scope of your project.
Which parts of the business problem will be addressed?
What are the project goals?

The current implementation of the Buddhist Church of Florin's website was created in Wordpress. The webpage is approximately 20 years old and has limited functionality.
The goal of the project is to make the webpage more dynamic, and easier for the administrative staff to manage.

Solution

Give a high-level overview of the proposed solution.
Describe main product features.
You may include screenshots and/or videos walk-through of your project if needed.
Migrate the infrastructure of the website from Wordpress to MERN (Mongo DB, Express, React.js, Node.js) Stack.
Updated site will include features such as:

  • Administrative login where admin's of the church can change content
  • Google Calendar that the admin's can easily manage
  • Payment system where church members can pay membership dues
  • Updated User-Interface with more dynamic content
